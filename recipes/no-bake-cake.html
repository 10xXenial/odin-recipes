2 Ingredient No Bake Cake

Description

his cake is creamy, almost like a cheesecake texture. It is very rich and decadent. When the bananas are mixed with the melted chocolate, it prevents the chocolate from turning back into its solid form. Instead, the chocolate stays in a semi-solid state. So you end up with a rich, fudgy cake that is similar to a flourless chocolate cake.

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. First, you need to mash the bananas and then you need to melt the chocolate until smooth.
  2. The two are then stirred together until evenly combined.
  3. Your batter is then ready to be poured into a cake pan and placed into the fridge to set.
